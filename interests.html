Interests

This page highlights both my professional interests and the hobbies that inspire me outside of programming. From artificial intelligence and software development to video games, music, and movies, I enjoy exploring new technologies while pursuing creative projects that challenge me to keep learning.

Professional Interests

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence and machine learning are among my favorite areas of computer science. During my capstone at Berea College, I built Moodr, a PyTorch-based music emotion classifier that predicts whether a song sounds Happy, Angry, Calm, or Sad using MFCC audio features. I enjoy building models that can interpret audio or visual data and am excited to continue exploring machine learning and computer vision.

Software Engineering

I enjoy creating software that solves real problems and seeing an idea become a finished application. Whether I’m developing desktop tools, web applications, or experimenting with games and AI, I appreciate every stage of the development process.

Cybersecurity

Security is an essential part of software development. I’m particularly interested in network and cloud security because I want the applications and websites I build to be secure, reliable, and resistant to malicious attacks.

System Design

I’m interested in learning how to design systems that can support large numbers of users efficiently. While my projects have primarily been smaller in scale, I’m eager to gain experience building scalable and highly available applications.

Technology & Development

Web Development

Although I’ve worked on both frontend and backend development, I enjoy backend development the most. I like building the functionality that powers applications and am interested in learning frameworks such as Django, React, and Angular to become a stronger full-stack developer.

Open Source

I haven’t contributed to an open-source project yet, but it’s one of my long-term goals. I look forward to collaborating with other developers and giving back to the programming community.

Mobile Apps

Mobile development is an area I’m excited to explore. Now that I have access to Xcode, I hope to build cross-platform applications that reach both iOS and other devices.

Cloud Computing

Cloud platforms are becoming increasingly important in modern software development, and I’m eager to learn technologies such as AWS, Microsoft Azure, or Google Cloud. Understanding cloud infrastructure will help me build applications that are more scalable and reliable.

Personal Hobbies

Gaming

Video games are one of my biggest hobbies and a major reason I became interested in programming. I especially enjoy Soulsborne games, Hunt: Showdown, and indie titles like Hollow Knight and Terraria, and one of my long-term goals is to publish my own indie game.

Reading

I enjoy reading fiction and especially seinen manga. My favorite series is Tokyo Ghoul, and I enjoy stories with compelling characters and darker themes.

Fitness

Staying active helps me maintain a healthy balance outside of programming. After taking a weightlifting course in college, I now regularly practice bodyweight exercises such as push-ups, squats, sit-ups, and crunches.

Music

Music is a huge part of my everyday life. I primarily listen to metalcore, swancore, nu metal, alternative rock, grunge, and progressive metal, and I also enjoy playing guitar whenever I have the opportunity.

Creative Interests

Writing

While I don’t write professionally, I enjoy documenting projects and explaining technical concepts. Clear communication is an important skill that complements software development.

UI/UX Design

Design isn’t my strongest area, but I appreciate the importance of creating intuitive and accessible user experiences. I’m continually working to improve my frontend design skills.

Content Creation

I’ve always enjoyed creating content and hope to start producing more YouTube videos featuring funny gaming moments with friends. It’s a creative outlet that combines several of my interests.

Favorite Topics

Machine Learning

Machine learning fascinates me because it enables computers to recognize patterns and make intelligent decisions from data. I’m particularly interested in applications involving audio analysis and computer vision.

Data Science

I enjoy learning how data can be analyzed to uncover meaningful insights and improve machine learning models. Developing a stronger understanding of data science will complement my AI interests.

Game Development

Game development combines nearly everything I enjoy—programming, problem solving, creativity, and design. Although I’ve built several small games in college, my ultimate goal is to release games on Steam.

Human-Computer Interaction

Creating software that is intuitive and accessible is important to me. I believe applications and games should be designed so that as many people as possible can enjoy using them.
